Our Story

408 Boba is dedicated to serving delicious bubble tea and refreshing beverages to the local community. We strive to provide a welcoming environment where customers can enjoy quality drinks at affordable prices.

Our menu features a variety of classic and innovative boba tea flavors, made with care to ensure consistent taste and quality. We use fresh ingredients and offer customizable options to suit different preferences, from tea strength to sweetness levels.

At 408 Boba, we aim to create a friendly neighborhood spot where people can relax, connect with friends, or simply enjoy a moment of refreshment during their day. We're committed to providing excellent customer service and maintaining a clean, comfortable atmosphere for all our guests.

About 408 Boba

Our passion for authentic boba culture and community

Our Mission

408 Boba is a local boba tea shop committed to serving quality beverages in a friendly and welcoming environment. We believe that great drinks bring people together, and our mission is to create a space where customers can relax, connect with friends, and enjoy expertly crafted boba teas. We aim to provide delicious drinks, excellent customer service, and a comfortable atmosphere that makes every visit memorable.

Our Values

Quality: We use fresh, high-quality ingredients and maintain strict standards in preparing our drinks. From our carefully selected teas to our perfectly cooked tapioca pearls, we ensure consistent taste and quality in every cup we serve.

Community: We strive to be more than just a boba shop - we want to be a gathering place for our local community. Whether you're studying, meeting friends, or taking a break from your day, we aim to provide a welcoming space for everyone.

Service: Our team is dedicated to providing friendly, efficient, and personalized service. We take the time to understand your preferences and help you discover new flavors, ensuring every interaction is positive and memorable.

Variety: We offer an extensive menu with a wide range of flavors, toppings, and customization options. From classic milk teas to innovative seasonal creations, we cater to diverse tastes and dietary preferences, including dairy-free and sugar-free alternatives.

Our Team

Our passionate and friendly staff brings enthusiasm and expertise to every drink we prepare. Each team member is thoroughly trained in our preparation methods and committed to maintaining our high standards. We believe that great customer service starts with knowledgeable, caring staff who take pride in their work and genuinely enjoy helping customers discover their perfect drink.

Our Commitment

We are committed to continuous improvement and staying current with trends in the boba tea industry. We regularly update our menu with seasonal offerings and customer favorites, always seeking feedback to enhance our products and service. Our goal is to be your go-to destination for quality boba tea in a comfortable, friendly environment.

Visit Us

We look forward to welcoming you to 408 Boba and sharing our passion for great boba tea with you. Whether you're a longtime boba enthusiast or trying it for the first time, our team is here to guide you through our menu and help you find something delicious. Come experience the 408 Boba difference - we're confident you'll become a regular customer and part of our growing community.
